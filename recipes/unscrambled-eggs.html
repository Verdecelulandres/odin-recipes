Unscrambled eggs!

We are going to start with the simplest of all recipes. These are some soggy and salty spiced eggs on a bowl. This breakfast recipe is adored by children and adults alike due to its flavor and sensation in the mouth.

Some people might say:"Those are just raw eggs with some spices, that's no way to have breakfast. You guys are lazy" And althought that might be true is not laziness but effectiveness what makes chef Scratch's recipes great. My only concern here is how chef Scratch is getting these eggs. There ain't no chickens on the Antartic that I know of!

Ingredients

Well, Unscrambled eggs are really straight forward You'll just need the following 4 ingredients:

  • 2 eggs (Don't ask from where)
  • 1 cup of powdered red hot chilli pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of pepper

Preparation

  1. First you will crack ope the eggs on the designated bowl
  2. add the salt and pepper
  3. add the chilli powder
  4. DON'T SCRAMBLE

Following these steps you should be able to get a nutritional and quick breakfast.

Serve at room temperature!
